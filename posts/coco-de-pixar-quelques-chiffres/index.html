    Coco from Pixar : some numbers

    Coco from Pixar : some numbers

    General, TLDR

    This post summarizes some interesting facts mentionned in an interview given by Renee Tam from Pixar at Foundry (October, 25th 2017) 1.

    Coco movie 2 contains:

    • 8.7 millions lights
    • 27 millions objects
    • 1 billions assets

    The most challenging scenes required up to 450 hours of rendering per frame.

    By implementing some optimization techniques, in particular by pre-processing some backgrounds areas, they were able to accelerate rendering time to 55 hours per frame.
